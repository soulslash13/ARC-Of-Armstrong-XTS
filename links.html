Health Care Transition Resources

  1. Got Transition
    • This is a great website to explore the state of the are resources to assist with medical transition planning.
  2. Young Women's Health and Young Men's Health
    • Sponsored by Boston's Children's Hospital, there sites offer topic based information on a wide variety of health topics related to young adult health.
  3. DHS Allegheny County Family Resource Guide
    • Contains multiple resources for medical and non-medical transition
    • Click on Transition for general Transition Information
    • Click on the Medical tab for Medical Transition Information including links to PA Dept. of Health Transition Checklist
  4. Florida HATS
    • Transition Website based in Florida - great general information and videos to assist in transition planning
