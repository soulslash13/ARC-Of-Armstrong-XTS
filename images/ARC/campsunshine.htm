Camp Sunshine '09

Camp Sunshine will be held June 22, 23, & 24(Mon.-Wed.) from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m. at Crooked Creek Park. Camp Sunshine is a three day; day camp for individuals with intellectual disabilities. Each day is filled with activites and entertainment.

Volunteers Needed-Leo Club, National Honor Society, Student Council, Boy Scouts, Girl Scouts etc. We have volunteers from all of the schools and community service oranizations. If you are interested call the Arc of Armstrong at (724)545-3426.
